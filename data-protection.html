Data Protection

Data Protection

As a freelance 3D and VFX artist

As a freelance 3D and VFX artist, I treat all client data, project files, and creative materials with the highest level of confidentiality and care. I understand that many projects involve sensitive content, proprietary designs, or unreleased visual material.

Any information, files, or communication shared with me will not be passed on to third parties and will only be used for the purposes of the agreed project. All data is stored securely and handled in accordance with current data protection regulations (such as the GDPR, where applicable).

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, or if you require a confidentiality agreement (NDA), feel free to reach out.
